Bay Area Deep Learning School lectures (Stanford)

2016-09-25
Artificial Intelligence > Deep Learning > Learning > Machine Learning

The Deep Learning School is a great two day set of introduction lectures on Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision for Deep Learning. The two days event is solely dedicated to Deep Learning! The lectures take place at CEMEX auditorium at the Graduate School of Business on Stanford campus (CA, USA) and include a variety of prominent speakers from the above mentioned research fields.

In my opinion, the most interesting lecturers of the first day were the ones of Andrej Karpathy with title “Deep Learning for Computer Vision“, Ruslan Salakhutdinov with title “Foundations of Deep Unsupervised Learning” and Andrew Ng with title “Nuts and bolts of applying deep learning“.

Day two video stream** **is still on air and will be available at the end of the event.

  • Day 1 on YouTube.
  • Day 2 on YouTube.

It is unique opportunity to learn from the renown Machine Learning experts. It is a must see for anyone who is interested in Deep Learning.
